Amazon Flex for local retailers

Driver

  • Courier sets working hours for the day.
  • Order pickups and drop-offs are logged.
  • Every order delivered and mile tracked based on routing instructions earn extra revenue.
  • Weekly earnings are deposited in the couriers preferred payment method, such as ACH, PayPal or Cash.

Store

  • Logging store inventory and adding important details like pricing, description as well as the other variants and quantities available.
  • Order Items are collected. If an item is missing or damaged, its status is updated and it is automatically removed from the site.
  • Items are packaged into the Comet branded poly-mailer.
  • The delivery label is printed using the handheld photo-printer and stuck on the package.

Shop

  • Users must create accounts (upon first checkout), only email password and address necessary.
  • Orders are received in our Comet branded poly-mailer.
  • Returns are scheduled inside the app for a Comet driver to pick them up.

Admin

  • We can change the status of orders, look at real-time location of couriers and their routes
  • Payments is a big aspect of this and we sort them by date, payments received for orders, refunds scheduled and driver payouts
  • A lot of work left for the admin page.
